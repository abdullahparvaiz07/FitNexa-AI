I've been feeling really sore after my heavy leg day yesterday. How should I optimize my rest day nutrition to help with recovery without going over my calorie goals?

smart_toy

Great question. Soreness (DOMS) after a heavy leg day requires targeted nutrients, not necessarily more calories. Here's how to optimize your recovery nutrition today:

  • check_circle Prioritize Protein Quality: Ensure you hit at least 1.6g-2.0g per kg of body weight today. Focus on fast-absorbing protein (like whey or lean chicken) in your first meal.
  • check_circle Anti-inflammatory Foods: Incorporate Omega-3s (salmon, chia seeds) and antioxidants (berries, dark leafy greens) to help reduce muscle inflammation.
  • check_circle Hydration & Electrolytes: Drink extra water today and add a pinch of sea salt or a low-calorie electrolyte powder to help flush out metabolic waste.
